About Us

Established in 2004, the company has been successfully catering to the clients of flexible packaging material, both in roll form and pouch form.

Founded by Mr. C.M.Sharma, a 1990 batch alumnus of Indian Institute of Packaging in Mumbai, the company has been moving steadily upwards while providing best quality laminates. 2 or 3 layer laminates are manufactured using various combinations of films – PET, LDPE, LLDPE, HDPE – in order to enhance the brand image and sustaining the quality of product to be packaged. With an annual production capacity of 1000 MT, Vision Packaging is well equipped to cater to the diverse needs of the industry.
